I am Katie. I am a full-stack junior developer living in Sydney.


As a graduate of General Assembly's Web Development Immersive program, I approach code with a think-outside-the-box mindset. I am driven by curiousity and finding out how things work, which has led to a life-long love of learning.


I love building creative applications that solve real-world problems. I am passionate about teaching children to code and increasing female participation in STEM.


When I'm not deep-diving into code, you can find me collecting houseplants, practising yoga, roller-skating, covered in ink from typography, or snuggled on a couch with a good book.


Let's chat and work together!
